About Me

I am Pranav Kishan, a 14 year old teen, who loves computers and programming. I am a Python backend developer, although I am now getting some experience on the front of the stack, using React Native and PyQt5, along with MongoDB. I also do electronics, as well as some game development as an hobby using C# and Unity.

If I am not programming or studying, I while away my time reading sci-fi novels, or playing video games and streaming them from a variety of genres, from E-sports shooters to sandbox games like Minecraft. 

Achievements

I am an top contributor at Nexus Aurora, an international community, dedicated to open sourcing Mars exploration.
Nexus Aurora is also a contestant for the Mars City State Design competition, held by the Mars Society. It is a massive undertaking, with over 200 people actively contributing every day. It is lead by Adrian Moisa, the founder of The Visual School.
Within it, I am the head of the access management project, which undertakes the task of managing access along the colony using computers and code.

Recipient of the Github Student developer Pack.
